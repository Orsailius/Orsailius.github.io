This week's patch brings Devices to the game!

New!

  • New mechanic 'Devices'
  • Devices are long cooldown untargeted abilities
  • Can bring 1 device to battle
  • 4 new Devices
  • Scorn [Fire] - Floating proximity mine
  • Nova [Ice] - Ring of frost that freezes enemies
  • Blockade [Earth] - Place a wall of rock behind you
  • Bastion [Light] - Take and deal no damage for 3 seconds
  • Inventory screen to pick active device
  • Jumping animation added
  • Visual cooldown indicators for devices and weapons

Fixes

  • When the master client leaves, the AI no longer mimics the new master client

Changes

  • Boulder's cooldown has been lowered
  • Earthquake's cooldown and initial damage has been lowered
Test out the alpha version on the site today!
