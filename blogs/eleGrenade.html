This week's patch brings Grenades to the game!

New!

  • New item 'Grenades'
  • Grenades are thrown and explode after 4.5 seconds
  • Can bring 1 type of grenade to battle
  • Default 2 grenades per life
  • 5 new Grenades
  • Classic Grenade [Fire] - Simple explosive grenade
  • Chill Grenade [Ice] - Slows enemies and does a small amount of damage
  • Sticky Grenade [Earth] - Homing grenade that sticks to the first target it finds, explodes 6 seconds later
  • Flash Grenade [Light] - Blinds enemies for 4 seconds
  • Curse Grenade [Dark] - Deals a small amount of damage then curses enemies to take 25% damage for 5 seconds
  • Inventory screen to pick active grenade
  • Effects bar showing your current status effects

Fixes

  • Skill info popup no longer flickers

Changes

  • The crouch key is now 'Left Control'
Test out the alpha version on the site today!
