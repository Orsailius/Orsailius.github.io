Cemetery Fighter

Choose a hero and fight evil in a cemetery!
Create a hero from 5 available classes
  • Warrior
  • Priest
  • Archer
  • Enchantress
  • Assassin
Fight monsters in cemeteries in 3 different game modes
  • Challenge: Test your skills fighting through 50 levels filled with monsters and bosses
  • Freeplay: A sandbox mode letting you choose how you want to play
  • Endless: Battle an endless horde of monsters,to see how long you can last
Play with or against your friends in multiplayer, or take on the adventure solo! Earn Achievements, Items, Emblems and Skill Points to upgrade your heroes to become the Cemetery Fighter!
