This week's patch brings Challenges to the game!

New!

  • New Game Mode: "Challenge"
  • Play specific crafted challenges to test your skill
  • Both single and multiplayer
  • Challenges pit you or your team against AI opponents(some with unique skills)
  • 5 Difficulties
  • Easy, Normal, Hard, Crazy, Legendary
  • Unlock higher difficulties by defeating lower ones
  • Legendary also has its own level system
  • See how high of a Legendary level you can beat!
  • Difficulty also scales with number of players
  • Earn experience in Challenges just like in normal play

Fixes

  • The Basic AI was sometimes getting skills from the host

Changes

  • Lobby now has a button for creating games in Challenge mode
  • For Challenge games, the map name column displays the name of the Challenge
Test out the alpha version on the site today!
