This week's patch brings more Challenges to the game!

New!

  • Fighting Fire Challenge
  • Fight a Steamtank AI on the Volcano map
  • They are Immune to fire damage, and deal extra fire damage
  • Deep Space Dynamo Challenge
  • Defeat 2 Dynamo AIs on the Spaceship map

Fixes

  • Bridges are now more even with the ground on Icecap
  • Several cliffs have been steepened on Icecap to prevent getting stuck on their sides
  • Fixed navigation for AIs on Volcano so they hopefully won't get stuck
  • Fixed a bug where Bastion's walls were indestructible on Volcano
  • Players no longer become unlit in certain areas of the Spaceship map
  • Fixed a bug where challenges would fail to save the proper difficulty level beat

Changes

  • Optimized player movement to lower lag in some situations
