This week's patch brings a Tutorial level to the game!

New!

  • Single Player Tutorial level
  • Tutorial is built for players new to the genre, or people who like tutorials
  • Covers walking, running, jumping, crouching, interacting, shooting, and changing weapons
  • Custom objects such as Targets and Batteries to interact with
  • Full descriptive audio
  • Helper text and objectives to get you through it

Fixes

  • Disabled self standing shooting animation, gun was taking up too much screen space
  • Bug where crouch animations would play while standing and the reverse

Changes

  • Main menu has a Tutorial button to access the Tutorial now
  • Increased accuracy of physics to avoid false bullet misses
